How to Identify a Phishing Attack in the future

Always check the sender's email address and name!

Check the email format to give a hint about the legitimacy of the content shared

Verify shared links by hovering over the link to identify if the web address path is suspicious

Scan any attachments to the email, a simple online virus scanner can do the job!

Where possible, make a phone call, an attacker can always spoof your friends or co-workers name, a simple phone call can confirm the legitimacy of the email and information asked.
